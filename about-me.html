What an 'ediot' website...

It all begins with an idea. Maybe you want to launch a business. Maybe you want to turn a hobby into something more. Or maybe you have a creative project to share with the world. Whatever it is, the way you tell your story online can make all the difference.

For me, it all began with the realisation I didn’t even attempt to make a personal site yet, for someone who spends as much time as I have on the internet. I am shocked that it took me this long to make an online profile for myself. Hello! It’s me! Just another nerd on the internet.

Full disclose, that picture of me in Disneyland is quite old now. I look quite different and need to update it.

The cog that moved everything along was the fact that I wanted to make a small picture frame to stick on the ground next to Jen. When looking online to get something made in time for my next visit I accidentally came across a small QR code I can stick on the ground. I decided to make a whole webpage and stick it on a QR code, rather than use one small picture. I knew it would be a bit too much to make a site just for Jen, so I made this site. With the first page being dedicated to Jen's memory. I didn't get this idea done as it would have been quite costly at the time as I was just going to pay for a SquareSpace site. I changed my mind and decided to make this a better learning experience by hosting it on a GitHub page and making the site pretty much from scratch.
